How to Use This List

Start with your primary workflow:

  1. Podcast / interview / lecture → transcript-driven clipping + reframing
  2. Tutorial / webinar → chapter/segment detection + captions + formatting
  3. Gaming / streaming → event-based highlights + social export
  4. Enterprise / platform → APIs for shot detection + indexing + search

Then choose:

  • A SaaS tool if you want speed.
  • An OSS pipeline if you want control, privacy, and cost predictability.
  • A developer API if you're building a product.

Long-form → Short-form (Auto Highlights / Auto Clips)

Tools that turn long videos into multiple Shorts/Reels/TikToks with automatic highlight selection and formatting.

  • OpusClip (SaaS) — AI repurposing: highlights → short clips.
  • Klap (SaaS) — one-click long video → Shorts/Reels/TikTok.
  • Vizard (SaaS) — long-form → short clips, "remove bad takes" workflow.
  • Munch (SaaS) — AI video repurposing platform for extracting engaging clips.
  • Riverside Magic Clips (SaaS) — generates short clips from recordings; integrates into a recording workflow.
  • WayinVideo Long to Short (SaaS) — 10+ viral clips in 30 seconds with AI moment detection & smart layouts.
  • vidyo.ai / Quso (SaaS) — long-form → short-form repurposing & resizing workflows.
  • VEED Long Video to Short Video (SaaS) — repurpose long content into shorts.
  • Short AI (Long → Short) (SaaS) — automatic highlight selection for short-form outputs.

Transcript-First Editing (Talking-Head Acceleration)

Text-based editing, filler-word removal, and silence/bad-take cutting for talking-head content.

  • Descript – Remove Filler From Video (SaaS) — transcript-first editing + filler removal.
  • Gling (SaaS) — auto remove silences, filler words, bad takes.
  • auto-editor (OSS) — command-line auto-cut based on audio loudness.

Captions & Short-Form Packaging

Captioning, animated subtitles, and "viral caption" styling workflows.

  • Kapwing Subtitles (SaaS) — auto subtitles + export formats.
  • VEED AI Clip Generator (SaaS) — AI clip generation + web editor.
  • Submagic (SaaS) — captions + b-roll + short-form edits.
  • Zeemo (SaaS) — captions/subtitles workflows and related tooling.

Gaming & Live Highlights

Automatic stream/game highlight extraction and conversion to social formats.

  • Eklipse (SaaS) — AI gaming highlights; export to Shorts/Reels/TikTok.
  • Powder (SaaS) — AI clipping for gaming; social-ready highlights.
  • WayinVideo Gaming Clips (SaaS) — AI gaming clips with "Find Moments" search & viral score prediction.
  • Streamlabs Cross Clip (SaaS) — convert Twitch clips to TikTok/Shorts/Reels formats.

Developer APIs (Understanding / Indexing / Shot Detection)

For building products: shot/scene segmentation, indexing, search, and enrichment.

  • WayinVideo API (API) — automate viral clip creation with find moments, animated captions in 100+ languages & AI reframe.
  • Google Cloud Video Intelligence – Shot Detection (API) — shot change detection / annotation workflows.
  • AWS Rekognition Video – Segment Detection (API) — segment detection including shot detection.
  • Azure AI Video Indexer – Insights Overview (API) — transcripts, OCR, faces, topics, and more.
  • TwelveLabs – Search Guides (API) — video understanding/search for finding specific moments.
  • Cloudinary – Content-aware Video Cropping (API) — automated cropping to keep salient content.

Open-Source Building Blocks

Recommended OSS modules for assembling your own clipping pipeline.

Speech-to-Text / Alignment

  • Whisper (OSS) — multilingual ASR / translation.
  • faster-whisper (OSS) — Whisper via CTranslate2 for faster inference.
  • whisperX (OSS) — word-level timestamps + diarization workflows.
  • whisper.cpp (OSS) — C/C++ port optimized for local inference.

Shot / Scene / Segment Detection

  • PySceneDetect (OSS) — shot change detection + optional splitting.

Clip Generation / Repurposing

  • ClipsAI (OSS) — transcript-aware long video → clips; includes aspect ratio conversion.

Editing / Assembly

  • MoviePy (OSS) — Python video editing (cut/concat/composite).
  • auto-editor (OSS) — remove dead space/silence automatically.

Datasets & Benchmarks (Highlights / Summarization)

Useful for research, evaluation, or building custom highlight selection models.

  • TVSum — classic benchmark dataset for video summarization.
  • VideoXum — cross-modal video summarization benchmark (video + aligned text).
  • Mr. HiSum — large-scale highlight detection & summarization dataset.
